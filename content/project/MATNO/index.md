**MATNO** is a think & do tank that we are building to support **West Africa's development** through evidence-based research, innovation and digital transformation. Africa's demographic momentum is one of the defining forces of this century, and MATNO's mission is to turn data and research into actionable ideas for the region — starting with workforce and human capital questions.

On the technical side, the MATNO website is a modern, accessible **React/TypeScript** application featuring an interactive **Three.js 3D globe** that visualizes African workforce projections, with a mobile-first responsive design and WCAG-compliant accessibility. This project is a joint effort with my co-contributors [Bachir Sabo](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bachir-sabo-087b7a247/) (Data Science Engineer, The World Bank — [bachirsabo.com](https://bachirsabo.com)) and [Khalil-Jonathan Jewiti-Rigondza](https://www.linkedin.com/in/khalil-jonathan-j-9b69a0174/) (Biostatistician, Amaris Consulting).
